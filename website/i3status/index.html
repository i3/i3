i3status

i3status is a small program (less than 1000 SLOC) for generating a status bar for dzen2, xmobar or similar programs. It is designed to be very efficient by issuing a very small number of system calls, as one generally wants to update such a status line every second. This ensures that even under high load, your status bar is updated correctly. Also, it saves a bit of energy by not hogging your CPU as much as spawning the corresponding amount of shell commands would.

Development

i3status is currently developed at http://code.stapelberg.de/git/i3status. Checkouts of the master branch are intended to be stable and working all the time. Integration of new features happens in a separate branch.
